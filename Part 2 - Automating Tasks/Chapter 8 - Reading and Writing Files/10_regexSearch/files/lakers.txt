Los Angeles Lakers
-------------------
- Founded: 1947
- Championships: 17
- Notable Players: Magic Johnson, Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, Kobe Bryant, Shaquille O'Neal, LeBron James
- Arena: Crypto.com Arena
- Colors: Purple, Gold, Black
- Mascot: None

The Los Angeles Lakers are one of the most successful teams in NBA history, known for their storied rivalry with the Boston Celtics and a legacy of star players and championships.
