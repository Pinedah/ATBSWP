Chicago Bulls
--------------
- Founded: 1966
- Championships: 6
- Notable Players: Michael Jordan, Scottie Pippen, Dennis Rodman, Derrick Rose
- Arena: United Center
- Colors: Red, Black, White
- Mascot: Benny the Bull

The Chicago Bulls gained worldwide fame in the 1990s thanks to Michael Jordan, who led the team to six championships and established a dynasty that is still celebrated today.
