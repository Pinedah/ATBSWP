Golden State Warriors
----------------------
- Founded: 1946
- Championships: 7
- Notable Players: Wilt Chamberlain, Rick Barry, Chris Mullin, Stephen Curry, Kevin Durant
- Arena: Chase Center
- Colors: Royal Blue, Gold
- Mascot: None

The Golden State Warriors are known for their modern era dominance, winning multiple championships in the 2010s led by Stephen Curry, and revolutionizing the game with their three-point shooting prowess.
